Fair Play

Rummy game is a individual's skill not a team effort. Hence, as part of our Fair Play Policy the following aspects covered are to safeguard the true spirit of skill & competition in online rummy at The Rummy Room.

Your Chat Privileges

Certain forms and topics of chat are not allowed on The Rummy Room. Chat privileges of players who are engaging in offensive chat will be subject to a suspension. .

Profanity/Abuse

The Rummy Room prohibits profane, vulgar, racist or abusive/insulting chat in our chat windows.

Begging/Solicitation

The Rummy Room prohibits players to beg for chips , whether play money or real money. No solicitation is allowed in any rummy at any time.

Chip Sales

Players are not permitted to engage in any discussion regarding the sale of The Rummy Room chips within our chat facilities. Any such discussion, including the offer to add or sell chips in The Rummy Room chat, is grounds for suspension.

Flooding

The sending of multiple and frequent messages to the chat window in order to drown out legitimate chat is not allowed.

Commercial Use

It is strictly prohibited for a User to use Chat for any commercial use whatsoever, including making any statements which promote any service or product of any party except The Rummy Room.

Malicious Behaviour

It is strictly prohibited for a User to use Chat for any malicious or offensive behaviour, including collusion, fraud, spam, and/or harassment. Users shall not make statements about The Rummy Room that are untrue or would reasonably be considered to be derogatory or critical .

Live Chat

The Rummy Room does not actively monitor chat , and you play at tables with chat enabled at your own risk. You may not make any chat comments which may affect the play of any hand or tournament.

Report a Problem

We encourage players to help us be our eyes and ears. The cooperation of our players is a critical part of making The Rummy Room a fun experience for all. Please do not engage in retaliatory chat; as this will likely result in both users chat being suspended . Please report any of the above offenses to support@rummyroom.com

Your Username

Your username is a unique name that will identify you on The Rummy Room and will be seen by other players on the table. It is what you choose when you create an account and cannot be changed / modified. Your UserID is subject to rules and User Agreement and subject to suspension based on below

Profanity/Abuse

The Rummy Room prohibits profane, vulgar, racist or abusive/insulting UserID

Privacy

The Rummy Room ensures that for a User to use his/her UserID to disclose personal information like email address, web address, phone number and / or partial details leading to the same.

Commercial Use

It is strictly prohibited for a UserID on The Rummy Room to be used for any commercial use whatsoever, including which promote any service or product.

Other UserIDs

Certain forms and words are not allowed. For e.g. your UserID cannot contain The Rummy Room.

Security

The Rummy Room is committed to ensuring high standards of security for all transactions made. It has one of the most sophisticated tools of the industry to ensure your money is safe when you make a transaction with us and industry leading standards in detecting in-game fraud and collusion.

Secure Payments

All transactions on The Rummy Room are encrypted with 2048-bit SSL

All information in The Rummy Room is stored in a secure environment and is not shared with any third-party

Prompt response to any issue, if found

Continuous review of security measures

Fraud & Collusion Protection

Games on The Rummy Room are monitored round the clock to detect any fraudulent behavior, Anti-Collusion measures are undertaken before, during and after each game on The Rummy Room.

Before Game Starts Seating is random, and no seat is prefixed for any game. Players, therefore have no control over selection of players on any table on our platform.

To shuffle cards, the Random Number Generator used by The Rummy Room has been certified by iTech Labs, an IEC/ISO 17025 accredited testing laboratory.

Players logged in from the same IP address cannot be allocated seats on the same table.

In-Play

On The Rummy Room, information about the playing cards is always encrypted, thereby preventing any third party from viewing the same.

Our Anti-fraud algorithms track every move (selection of cards, discards, melds, shows) by every player on every table round the clock.

After Game ends

Players suspected of collusion are blocked from playing at the same table in future.
